Happiest Countries around the World

The term Happiness is used in the context of mental or emotional states, including positive or pleasant emotions ranging from contentment to intense joy. It is also used in the context of life satisfaction, subjective well-being, eudaimonia, flourishing and well-being.

How Happy are the Countries around the world?

Happiness Score

In this map, we look at the Happiness Score of all the countries in the year 2017 The map is divided into 4 parts Very Happy which has Happiness Score between 7 and 8, Happy which has Happiness Score between 6 and 7, Somewhat Happy which has Happiness Score between 5 and 6 and Not Happy which has Happiness Score below 5. Hover over a country to see the Happiness Score.

Happiness Score by Region

Below we see all the countries on the map, Hover over a dot to see the Happiness Score and Happiness Rank of the country. To see the counteries by region please use the Region Selection option and to see countries by their Happiness Status click on the status on the legend. Through this visualization we can see that the most happiest country in 2017 was Norway with a Happiness Score of 7.537.

North America has the highest average Happiness Score which is 7.15. While Europe is the least happiest region with a Happiness Score of 4.09 on average. It is interesting to note that the happiest country Norway is a part of Western Europe yet the region ranked 2nd.

What makes a country Happy?

Does Money buy Happiness?

A highly popular belief in our society is that money can buy you happiness. So most people would argue that a country's Economy is the number 1 factor in deciding if a country is happy or not. Below is a world map showing each country's Economy. Hover over a country to view the country's Economy, Happiness Score and Happiness Score.

Below is a bar chart showing the Top 5 Richest Countries. Hover over the graph to see the Economy and Happiness Score. It is interesting to see that even though these countries are very rich. They are not even in the top 10 happiest countries of the world.

Other factors?

As we see money is not the deciding factor in determining the happiness of a country.Other factors inclued Family, Health, Freedom, Generosity, Corruption, Dystopia and Job Satisfaction.
